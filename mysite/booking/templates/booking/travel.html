How to use

The first step is to sign up.

After successfully creating an account, you will be redirected to this page. You can now use the form above to search travels between two Spain cities.

What to expect

You will use a CRUD webapp. You can book a lots of tickets and check them in a dedicated page named MyTickets. It must appear in the top navbar after logging in.

You can also check and modify your profile information in Profile page.
